SERVICES

Services I provide to my clients primarily relate to the creation of websites and the ongoing maintenance they require to stay competitive.

When starting from scratch or revisiting an existing website, the process relies heavily on the clarification of goals and the timelines if applicable.

This approach gets modified as per the needs of the website or marketing campaign.

Below is my outline for the website creation process.

Ahhh the website creation process, where to start, what to consider, how much and how long!!!!????

I find the easiest approach to web development is a 7 step approach;

 Step 1.   Gathering Information: Purpose, Main Goals, and Target Audience

 Step 2.   Planning: Sitemap and Wireframe Creation

 Step 3.   Design: Page Layouts, Review, and Approval Cycle

 Step 4.   Content Writing and Assembly

 Step 5.   Coding

 Step 6.   Testing, Review, and Launch

 Step 7.   Maintenance: Opinion Monitoring and Regular Updating

Step 1. Gathering Information: Purpose, Main Goals, and Target Audience

 Estimated time:  < 2 weeks

 Objectives: 

Set goals for site

Define Target Audience

Determine timelines

To make sure I create the perfect website for you, it's very important we get a clear understanding of what you want to achieve with it.

Who do you want to attract to your site and what are your main goals?

This will help us define the scope of the project and figure out all the important details, like which features you need, how long it'll take, and what the budget is.

Step 2. Planning: Sitemap and Wireframe Creation

 Estimated time:  < 6 weeks

 Objectives: 

Create Sitemap Journey

Create Wireframe

Choose technology stack

Here I create a layout reference that gives you a sneak peek into what your website will look like.

The sitemap is like a map that shows how all the different parts of your website are connected.

A wireframe is like a blueprint that helps us plan out the user interface design without any distractions graphic elements.

Step 3. Design: Page Layouts, Review, and Approval Cycle

 Estimated time:  < 12 weeks

 Objectives:  

Create page layouts

Utilize client feedback

Make layout edits

At this stage I will upload your logo and program the color, pallete and any custom typography settings.

I will create a website layout that showcases your content in the best way possible. The layout is like a visual blueprint that represents the information structure and basic functionality of your website.

We'll use your branded colors, logos, place holder text and images to give you a sneak peek of what your website will look like.

Step 4. Content Writing and Assembly

 Estimated time:  < 15 weeks

 Objectives: 

Create or obtain all content

Prepare for migration

Your copy writing should capture the essence of what you want to say to your target audience and encourage them to interact with your site via clear call-to-actions. The content writing process includes crafting catchy headlines, writing new text, and proofreading to make sure everything is perfect.

Lastly, we confirm that all assets are approved and gathered before I start the coding phase.

Step 5. Coding

 Estimated time:  < 15 weeks

 Objectives: 

Build and Deploy

Add special features and interactivity

Employ SEO treatment

The chosen frameworks and CMS are implemented to make sure that the server can handle the installation and set-up smoothly.

Here I will implement the chosen frameworks and CMS to make sure your website runs smoothly on your server.

All the cool stuff that was designed during the mock-up and layout creation will be brought to life and tested to perfection.

I will optimize your website for SEO by tweaking things like titles, descriptions, image optimization and keywords to help it rank higher in search engines.

Plus, we'll make sure the code is valid and AODA Accessibly compliant - this is super important for SEO!

Step 6. Testing, Review, and Launch

 Estimated time:  < 4 weeks

 Objectives: 

Test 

Make final edits 

Final test and launch

We're almost there, time to test, test, test!

Testing your website is an important part of the process, and I'll go through every single link to ensure they all function and never break.

This includes checking every form and script, and running a spell-check to catch any possible typos.

Using code validators, I make sure your code follows current web standards - this is crucial because cross-browser compatibility is very important.

Once complete, it's time to upload your website to a server and make it live for the world to see.

Step 7. Maintenance: Opinion Monitoring and Regular Updating

 Estimated time:  Ongoing

 Objectives: 

Add user report system

Eradicate any found bugs from live user experience

Develop maintenance plan

Although optional, adding a user report intake form to your new site will allow users to give feedback, and assist detecting any problems they may experience, unique to their browser or device.

Continual in-browser and on-mobile testing will eradicate any pesky bugs that might pop up to ensure your website is running smoothly.
Finally, we'll develop a maintenance plan to keep your website up-to-date and running smoothly for the long-term.

Remember, your website is more than just a product - it's a service that you're providing to your clients.

I want to make sure it's the best service possible and your website is always up and running perfectly!
