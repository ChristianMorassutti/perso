YOU

Firstly, I love all of my clients.

Every client is unique and represents a world of opportunity for me. This is why I dedicate myself to my clients success. 

I depend on my clients just like they depend on me, and when we both commit to the project, I find tasks become easier to accomplish and all of our goals become clearer faster!

Below are some traits I REALLY value in clients, I work by these same principles and urge clients to truly OWN their role in the business relationship through these principles. Trust me, no-one is perfect but a bit of consideration does go a long way!

 Engaged:   Interested in the betterment of the website and is actively engaged in the improvement of the website's content.

 Collaborative:   Offering insights on or falls within, the website's target audience, and is likely to have a genuine interest in the products or services offered. Also, be able to collaborate with me the website developer, sharing ideas and providing feedback in a constructive manner.

 Responsive:   Simply responding to communication in a timely manner that won't disrupt or hold up the process.

 Clear Communicator:   Includes being responsive, available, and providing clear instructions and feedback.

 Respectful:   Values expertise and time. This includes paying on time, communicating professionally and acknowledging the effort and skills of the web developer.

 Realistic:   Regarding expectations, the website development process, and the results that can be achieved within the budget and timeline.

 Organized:   Providing the necessary content and materials in a timely manner, allowing the website developer to meet the project's deadlines.
 Open-minded:   Open to new ideas and willing to take risks, allowing the web developer to be creative and deliver innovative solutions.

To conclude, the working relationship is after all, a relationship. The effort must be appreciated, collaboration should be welcomed and ultimately teamwork must be nurtured throughout the project. I trust in these attributes and deliver nothing but the best, if you share this feeling with me perhaps you are my next AMAZING client?!?!
