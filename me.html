MEET ME

My story begins in Toronto, I had an opportunity to volunteer in the "Devs Room" of a large and successful charity to sit-in and learn. I was eager to learn web development as digital commodity that I could grow with in both knowledge and success. 

Let's just say the team was a "lively bunch" of geniuses who stopped at nothing to help me grasp the tools and methodologies essential to their work. Soon after, it was decided that should I enrol in a course that would show me the basics of HTML CSS and Javascript, I would have a good shot of making it on the team!

Using Jira to handle the incoming tickets for e-blast creation or content/page updates I stayed on for 3 years, working with the best team, liaising with department heads and powerful marketing teams, doing everything we could for the causes we loved supporting.

This experience formed my web development foundation and I'm really proud of it!

I learned that communication is paramount for clearly and empathically identifying motivations and strengths among team members especially, but also understanding the values of external stake holders.

I learned that processes can always improve if you care about the steps taken and the people you work with.

I learned that ownership and responsibility to improve is individual and success is built-in through the support and guidance of true leadership.

I studied at Juno College ( formerly HackerYou ) learning the most up-to-date semantic HTML5 coding, styling with CSS3 and getting my feet wet in Vanilla Javascript. I had prior in-depth understanding of web development from studying Integrated Media at OCADU ( formerly Ontario College of Art and Design ) however things came a long way from my studies with Adobe Dreamweaver and Flash programming.

With some "rookie luck" and determination, I learned fast, everything I could about the world of CMS builders specifically WordPress. I was also introduced to the mighty marketing email or "E-blast, with a build process including Adobe Photoshop and Pardot softwares and deployment through Salesforce. 

Yes I have also continued to learn technical knowledge, new languages, software solutions and best practices that surround the betterment of web 2.0; Accessibility built in, responsive client experiences and sustainable ownership of web properties.

Moving forward, I bring heart and personality to teamwork, honesty and appreciation to client communications and work really hard for completion and satisfaction in every regard to every role I play for every single contract. You will notice this when we discuss working together in the near future ;)
